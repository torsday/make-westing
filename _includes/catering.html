CATERING



All catering orders must be placed six days prior to your event. There is a minimum of 15 per order and 20 for ceviche. You do not have to place the order for your entire party. For example, a party of 50 could order 25 chicken sliders and 25 duck sliders rather than 50 of each. Please select the items you wish to order, the amount you wish to order them for, and the time you would like to begin food service. Cold items will be served first and hot items will follow in rounds. We have chafing dishes to keep the food hot and a table will be set aside for your group. We do not have cocktail servers. Outside food is not allowed during the hours our kitchen is open (4pm-9pm everyday). However, as we do not currently offer any dessert options, you are welcome to bring your own dessert at no additional fee. Please feel free to contact the above email with any questions regarding the menu/private events. We look forward to hosting your party at Make Westing!


Menu is subject to change without notice due to seasonality or availability of ingredients
